    Services

    Compassionate Care Mobile Health

    Compassionate Care Mobile Health will provide medical services, psychiatric services, and social services. Medical services available will include primary care services, such as disease education and management, prescription refills, basic wound care, and immunizations. A psychiatrist will be available to provide assessment and treatment, including prescription refills, for individuals with mental illness. Social workers will be available to ensure patients are educated on available resources and to provide assistance enrolling in appropriate programs. These programs may include enrolling in medical benefits, drug and alcohol rehab, securing housing, and access to food.
