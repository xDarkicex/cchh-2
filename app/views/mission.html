Mission

Compassionate Care Mobile Health

Our mission is to provide medical care, via a mobile health clinic, to people whom society has neglected despite their great need, with a distinct focus on those passing through various stages of homelessness. Our hope is that through exposure, education and empathy - this beautiful mission will enable our recipients to utilize the tools they receive in order to respect and maintain their health and stay well. Finally, our wish is for this project to promote holistic healing, to provide a mobile clinic that the uninsured and underserved can visit to find peace through the power of medical sciences, dignity and compassion.
